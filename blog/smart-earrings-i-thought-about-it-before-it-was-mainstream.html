Smart Earrings: I Thought About It Before It Was Mainstream

June 3, 2025 · Reha Discioglu

Now that Sam & Jony are rumored to be working on smart earrings, just a gentle reminder: I thought about it before it was mainstream. 😁

The idea first came to me after seeing the Humane AI Pin and other pendant-style wearables aimed at similar use cases. While the technology was compelling, the form factor wasn’t. These devices were just too noticeable.

That’s the same problem most AI glasses have faced. When a device makes itself the center of attention, it breaks the subtlety that everyday interactions rely on. As a user, you don’t want the first thing someone notices about you to be the device you’re wearing.

That’s why I find the new Meta Ray-Bans interesting – they’re starting to solve this by integrating tech into familiar, socially accepted forms.

Overall, I’m excited to see the conversation shifting from raw tech to thoughtful design and I am hoping that we start seeing some beautifully designed digital earrings soon.

Smart Earrings: Blends seamlessly into daily life, unnoticed in casual interactions. Modular design allows for customizable tech – detach mic, camera, or speaker as needed. Wear full-featured or stripped down, adapting to the day’s requirements.
